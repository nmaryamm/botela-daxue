# Project Title: Disaster Resilience AI - FloodGuardAI Mobile Application (non-tech)

## Project Description:
FloodGuard is a flood early‑warning and emergency support application designed to help communities respond more effectively to flood disasters. The platform collects environmental data such as rainfall levels and historical flood information to estimate flood risk and provide early alerts to users.

The application helps residents stay informed about potential flood risks, locate nearby evacuation centres, access safety guidelines, and quickly contact emergency services through an SOS feature. By providing timely information and easy communication with rescuers, FloodGuard aims to reduce response time and improve community safety during flood emergencies.

The system is designed for people living in flood‑prone areas, as well as emergency responders and local authorities who need quick access to disaster information and coordination tools.

## Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities
FloodGuard improves disaster preparedness and helps protect vulnerable communities by providing early flood warnings and evacuation guidance.

SDG 13 – Climate Action
The platform helps communities adapt to climate-related disasters by using data-driven predictions and early alert systems.

SDG 17 – Partnerships for the Goals
FloodGuard encourages collaboration between communities, government agencies, and emergency responders to improve disaster response and management.

## Target Users
1. Flood Victims / Residents
People living in flood-prone areas across ASEAN countries such as Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, and Vietnam. They need fast alerts and clear information about floods, including what actions to take and where to go for safety. The app is designed to be simple so everyone can use it, including elderly people and families.

2. Rescue and Response Teams
Emergency teams such as firefighters, police, civil defence, and NGO volunteers. They need tools to monitor flood situations, see updates from communities, and coordinate rescue and aid more effectively during flood emergencies.
